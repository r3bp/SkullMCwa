Server Rules

This is our official server rules. Please read! Ignorance does not protect against punishment.

Home

Discord Rules

  1. Respect Everyone: Respect everyone regardless of their background or beliefs.
  2. No Harassment: Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination are strictly prohibited.
  3. Relevant Discussions: Keep conversations relevant to their respective channels.
  4. No Spamming: Avoid spamming or excessive self-promotion.
  5. Appropriate Language: Use appropriate language and maintain a respectful tone.
  6. Privacy Respect: Do not share personal information without consent.
  7. Follow Discord Guidelines: Adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  8. No NSFW Content: Sharing NSFW content or engaging in inappropriate discussions is not allowed.
  9. Listen to Moderators: Follow instructions from server moderators and administrators.

Minecraft Rules

  1. No Cheating: Using external or internal modifications to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited (e.g., AutoClicker).
  2. No Exploiting Bugs: Report any bugs or glitches to staff immediately, and do not exploit them.
  3. Voice Chat Conduct: Maintain general etiquette in voice chat platforms associated with the server.
  4. No Offensive Content: Offensive usernames or inappropriate content are not allowed.
  5. No Boosting/Free Kills: Avoid exploiting any methods to gain unfair kills or advantages.
  6. No Server Disruption: Do not attempt to disrupt or crash the server in any manner.
  7. No Harassment: Spamming, disruptive behavior, or harassment in chat or voice channels is prohibited.
  8. Respectful Communication: Communicate respectfully and avoid verbal abuse or harassment.
  9. No Inappropriate Language: Offensive, hateful, or discriminatory language is not permitted.
  10. No Sharing Private Info: Sharing personal information about yourself or others is forbidden.
  11. No Discrimination: Discrimination based on personal attributes is strictly prohibited.
  12. No Political/Religious Discussions: Focus on in-game activities and avoid sensitive topics.
